Use WiFi Whenever Possible

Connecting to WiFi whenever possible is a recommended practice to conserve battery life on your iPhone. WiFi generally uses less power than a cellular connection. Here's a concise version of your information:

Low Power Mode, introduced several years ago, remains a top choice for users concerned about maximizing their iPhone's battery life. This mode is designed to reduce background activities, such as automatic downloads, and accelerates the dimming of the display after periods of inactivity.

To maximize battery life on iPhone:

Connect to WiFi whenever possible, especially at home or work, as it saves cellular data and helps preserve battery life.
