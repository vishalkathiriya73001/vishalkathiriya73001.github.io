Disable Vibrations and Haptic Feedback

The haptic feedback and vibration settings on your iPhone can be a practical step to conserve battery life, especially for users who can do without these tactile responses. Here's a concise version of your information:

To disable haptic feedback and vibration on iPhone::
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to "Sounds & Haptics" (or a similar section depending on your iOS version).
  3. Toggle off the switches for "Vibrate on Ring" and "Vibrate on Silent."
  4. Adjust or disable other haptic feedback options as needed.
