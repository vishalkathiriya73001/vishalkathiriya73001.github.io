Manage Apps That Are Draining Battery

iPhone provides users with information about which apps are consuming the most battery, allowing them to identify potential culprits for excessive power consumption. Here's a condensed version of your information:

To check battery usage on iPhone:
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap on "Battery."
  3. Review the list to see which apps are using the most battery.

By checking battery usage statistics, users can pinpoint apps that may be contributing to excessive power drain, enabling them to take appropriate actions to optimize battery life.
