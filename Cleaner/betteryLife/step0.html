Limit When and How Often Apps Access Your Location

It's a good idea to check your location settings to limit apps accessing your location for privacy reasons, but it can also be beneficial to your battery life. Here's how to get to your Location Services settings:

  1. Open up the Settings app.
  2. Choose Privacy.
  3. Tap Location Services.
  4. Review the list and edit settings by tapping on the name of each app in the list.

You have four possible choices for location settings for each app, though not all four choices will always be available for every app depending on what it does. You can select the following: Never, Ask Next Time, While Using the App, and Always.

Preventing an app from accessing your location is crucial for privacy and battery efficiency. The options for controlling location access on most devices include:

Never:

Choosing "Never" prevents the app from accessing your location under any circumstances. This is the most privacy-conscious option and is suitable for apps that don't need location information to function.

Ask Next Time:

With this setting, the app will prompt you with a popup the next time it wants your location. You can then choose to temporarily approve it. This option is useful if you want to grant location access on a case-by-case basis.

While Using the App:

This option allows the app to detect your location only when it is actively open and in use. When you close the app or switch to another one, location access is terminated. It provides a balance between functionality and privacy.

Always:

Allowing an app to access your location at all times, whether the app is open or closed, is the least privacy-friendly option. It may lead to increased battery drain. Reserve this option for apps that genuinely require constant access, such as mapping or location-based services.

It's important to review and adjust these settings for each app, especially since some may request location information unnecessarily. For example, a banking app might ask for location access to show nearby ATMs, even though this information can be provided by entering a zip code. By clearing unnecessary location permissions, you can enhance your privacy and conserve battery life.
