Adjust Mail Fetch Settings

Adjusting the settings related to how often the Mail app checks for new emails can contribute to conserving battery life on your iPhone. Here's a concise version of your information:

To adjust Mail app settings for battery life:
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Scroll down and tap on "Mail."
  3. Under the "Mail" settings, select "Accounts."
  4. Choose the email account for which you want to adjust settings.
  5. Tap on "Fetch New Data."
  6. Adjust the settings, choosing options like "Manually," "Hourly," or customize fetch schedules for better battery efficiency.

By customizing these settings, you control how often the Mail app fetches new emails, optimizing battery usage based on your preferences and usage patterns.
