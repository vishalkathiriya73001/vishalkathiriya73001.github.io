Limit Apps Using Bluetooth

iOS 13 introduced a feature that informs users when apps request Bluetooth access, providing transparency about the apps that utilize Bluetooth for various purposes. This includes functions such as location tracking with Bluetooth beacons or scanning for devices like Chromecast. Here's a succinct version of your information:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap Privacy.
  3. Tap Bluetooth.

In iOS 13, users are notified when apps request Bluetooth access, revealing the numerous apps that utilize Bluetooth for functions such as location tracking or device scanning. This feature enhances user awareness and control over Bluetooth permissions.

Review the list of apps requesting Bluetooth access and toggle off access for any app that doesn't require Bluetooth functionality. If a feature within an app stops working properly, Bluetooth access can be re-enabled as needed.
