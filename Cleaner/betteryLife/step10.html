Limit Use of Widgets

widgets that require frequent updates, such as those related to weather or geo-location, can contribute to battery drain. If you have many widgets on your Home Screen or in the Today View, managing or removing some of them can be a practical strategy. Here's a concise version:

Remove Unnecessary Widgets:
  1. Press and hold on an empty area of your Home Screen until the apps jiggle.
  2. Tap the "+" icon in the top-left corner to enter the widget gallery.
  3. Find the widget you want to remove and tap on it.
  4. Select "Remove Widget" to take it off your Home Screen.

By reducing the number of widgets, especially those that require frequent updates, you can potentially improve battery life on your device.
